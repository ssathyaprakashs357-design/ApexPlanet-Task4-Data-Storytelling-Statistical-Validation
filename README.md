## Statistical Validation

### Null Hypothesis (H₀)

There is no significant difference in sales across product categories.

### Alternative Hypothesis (H₁)

There is a significant difference in sales across product categories.

---

## Key Insights

- Electronics generated the highest sales.
- Kolkata recorded the highest city-wise sales.
- Sales peaked during March, June, October, and November.
- Customer distribution remained nearly balanced.
- Dashboard insights support informed business decisions.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Increase inventory for high-performing products.
- Strengthen promotions during peak sales periods.
- Improve marketing strategies for lower-performing categories.
- Expand successful sales strategies to additional markets.
- Continuously monitor KPIs for business optimization.
